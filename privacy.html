Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 March 2026

Draw & Learn ("the App") is developed by Roberto ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require an account, does not track users, and does not use analytics or advertising SDKs.

Drawings and Content

Drawings created in the App are saved locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to any server or shared with us or any third party.

Photos

If you choose to save a drawing to your Photos library, the App will ask for permission first. This permission is used solely to save the image you created. We do not access, read, or upload any other photos on your device.

In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional subscription managed by Apple through the App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information, billing details, or Apple ID data.

Children's Privacy

Draw & Learn is designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users. The App contains no advertising, no tracking, and no social features.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services. The only external communication is with Apple's servers for in-app purchase processing.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties because we do not collect any user data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

COPPA Notice (Children Under 13)

Draw & Learn is directed to children under the age of 13. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require registration or account creation.
  • No behavioural tracking, advertising, or data sharing occurs.
  • Parents or guardians may contact us at any time to inquire about, review, or request deletion of any information relating to their child's use of the App.

Because the App collects no personal information, there is no user data for us to provide, correct, or delete — but we welcome any parental enquiries.
