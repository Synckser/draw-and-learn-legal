Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app, tap the lock icon to open the subscription screen, then tap Restore Purchases. Your purchase will be restored automatically if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. Draw & Learn collects no personal data. All drawings are saved on your device only and are never uploaded. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What ages is this app for?

Draw & Learn is designed for children ages 2–7, with guided drawing, tracing, and vocabulary activities.

The app isn't working correctly

Try these steps:

  • Make sure your device is running iOS 16 or later
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, email us with a description of the problem
